MOWOG Membership

We welcome all new and returning members!

MOWOG is an active club for enthusiasts of Minis, new and old alike.

Membership Benefits

  • MOWOG is an NZ Motorsport affiliated club
  • Monthly "Coffee run" drives
  • Monthly club nights in a relaxed and fun environment
  • Monthly club magazine with updates and club news
  • An active and helpful Facebook Group and community

Membership Options

Single Membership - $50 / year

Family Membership - $70 / year

Notes:

  • Family membership covers a member plus a partner living in the same house (and any children).
  • A portion of the membership fee covers the affiliation to Motorsport New Zealand

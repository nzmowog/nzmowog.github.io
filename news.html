British Car Day

We had a great turn out of cars that attended this years British Car day at Trentham.
Many members took along their minis to display. Lloyd and Linley brought along their cream mini and borrowed their sons blue mini. To their amazement, their sons mini (MYMNEE) won the people choice award for the event!
Well done guys, hopefully you won't have to put your new roadside breakdown kit to use anytime in the near future.

2017 Pork Pie Charity Run

This Easter 2017 60 Minis will muster in Kaitaia for the fifth running of the Pork Pie Charity Run. After 6 days and 7 nights on the road only the best prepared , the fittest, the strongest, the desperate, cunning, and the most courageous, will arrive in Invercargill. Over 2,500 Kms of New Zealand’s peripatetic roads must be travelled. You will encounter some of New Zealand’s biggest pot holes, wettest valleys, climb through cold mountain passes, cruise long South Island straights, pass through our biggest cities and friendliest rural towns. And all this time you will be mesmerised by this country's unique and ever-changing scenery from the front seat of a Mini.

22nd Mini Nationals 2016 - Otago

This year the Mini Nationals were hosted by the Otago Mini Owners Club Inc on 21st, 22nd, 23rd October 2016
Six MOWOG members in three minis attended the Otago Mini Nationals this year. It was a great weekend with around 100 cars parked in the Octagon for the Show and Shine event on the Saturday.
MOWOG can 3rd overall for the weekend which was a great result. Thanks to Otago Mini Club for hosting a fantastic weekend.
